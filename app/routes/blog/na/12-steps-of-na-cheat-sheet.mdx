# 12 Steps of NA - The Ultimate Cheat Sheet
<PostImage image={'https://res.cloudinary.com/recovery-ocean/image/upload/w_701/v1655939409/carousel-gec9c575fb_640_bsfoo4.png'} by="Image by 200 Degrees" alt="List on a computer screen" posted="Jun 26, 2022"/>

> Everything you need to know about the 12 Steps of NA in one place!

The cornerstone of the NA program is the 12 Steps. Whether you're new to NA or a veteran member, this cheat sheet will help you along your journey of working the 12 Steps.

Here it is, **'12 Steps of NA - The Ultimate Cheat Sheet'**.

## The 12 Steps of NA

### Step 1  
> We admitted we were powerless over our addiction, that our lives had become unmanageable.

* **Keywords:** admitted, powerless, unmanageable

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">This step is all about **acceptance**. We accept that we **cannot stop** using drugs on our own. Then, we **acknowledge** that our life has become **out of control**.</div>

### Step 2  
> We came to believe that a Power greater than ourselves could restore us to sanity.

* **Keywords:** came to believe, Power greater than ourselves, restore, sanity

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">After accepting that we cannot stop using drugs on our own, we **start** to believe that a **higher power** can help us.</div>

### Step 3  
> We made a decision to turn our will and our lives over to the care of God as we understood Him.

* **Keywords:** turn over, will and lives, care of God, understood

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">In this step, we **relinquish** control of our will to the **higher power** we came to believe in.</div>

### Step 4  
> We made a searching and fearless moral inventory of ourselves.

* **Keywords:** searching, fearless, moral inventory

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">Step 4 is a **deep dive** into our psyche. We examine things like our fear, pride, relationships, and tendencies so that we can **uncover** character flaws and take **responsibility** for our behavior.</div>

### Step 5 

> We admitted to God, to ourselves, and to another human being the exact nature of our wrongs.

* **Keywords:** admitted, God, ourselves, another human, exact nature, wrongs

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">In this step we take **responsibility** for the **wrongs** we have committed and **unburden** ourselves by sharing these dark secrets.</div>

### Step 6  

> We were entirely ready to have God remove all these defects of character.

* **Keywords:** entirely ready, God, remove, defects of character

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">Now that we've uncovered our character flaws and taken responsibility for our behavior, we are **ready** for our **higher power** to **remove** these flaws from us.</div>

### Step 7  

> We humbly asked Him to remove our shortcomings.

* **Keywords:* humbly, remove, shortcomings

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">In this step, we **reach out** to our higher power and **humbly** ask that he remove our character **defects**.</div>

### Step 8  

> We made a list of all persons we had harmed, and became willing to make amends to them all.

* **Keywords:** list, all persons, harmed, willing, amends

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">In step 8, we make a **list** of the people we have **wronged** and become **ready** to make amends to them.</div>

### Step 9  

> We made direct amends to such people wherever possible, except when to do so would injure them or others.

* **Keywords:** direct amends, except, injure, them or others

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">In this step, we make **amends** to those people we have **harmed**. We do this **face to face** wherever possible. However, we **refrain** from making amends if doing so would **harm** another person or ourselves.</div>

### Step 10  

> We continued to take personal inventory and when we were wrong promptly admitted it.

* **Keywords:** continued, personal inventory, promptly admitted

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">In this step, we adopt a practice of **monitoring** our tendencies and behaviors and **immediately admitting** any wrongdoings.</div>

### Step 11 

> We sought through prayer and meditation to improve our conscious contact with God as we understood Him, praying only for knowledge of His will for us and the power to carry that out.

* **Keywords** prayer, meditation, improve, contact with God, His will

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">Step 11 is about maintaining **contact** with our higher power praying for the ability to **understand** his will for us and the **ability** to act on it.</div>

### Step 12  

> Having had a spiritual awakening as the result of these steps, we tried to carry this message to addicts, and to practice these principles in all our affairs.

* **Keywords:** spirtual awakening, carry this message, practice, principles

<div className="rounded-md bg-slate-100 p-4 mt-4">This step is about **sharing** hope and the recovery experience with addicts who need help. We continue to live a life shaped by the **principles** we've learned through the **12 steps of NA**.</div>

These steps lay out a path for addicts to achieve freedom from drug dependency. 

Thousands of addicts worldwide have changed their lives by working these steps.
